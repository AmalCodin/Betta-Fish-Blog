Most Essential Betta Fish Products:



API Aquarium Salt:



Aquarium salt is a very very useful product for a betta fish owner because it has a vareity of uses and helps to cure many diseases that betta fishes get. It helps to kill many of the parasites that are in your aquarium. Many external parasites that attack betta fishes can be killed with the help of aquarium salt.

Giving your fish a salt water bath once a while can be very healthy for your fish. The best brand of aquarium salt available in the market till date is the API Aquarium salt which is the most famous out of all the brands.

If your betta fish is suffering some disease read my in detail article explaining all about betta fish diseases and how to cure them Betta Fish Diseases.

The amazon link for this product is here API Aquarium Salt.



Gravel Cleaner



This is the next important thing you must have for your aquarium because it is important to take out the uneaten food that remains in the aquarium because this can lead to many diseases. With the help of gravel cleaner we can have a full clean aquarium in which your betta fish will be very happy.

We can use the gravel cleaner while doing water changes. However, make sure to be careful while using them in your betta tank because if we put full vacum force into the tank and the betta fish gets sucked up by the gravel vacum it can be very stressful for your betta fish and it won't eat food so we must be very careful while using a gravel syphon.

It is always better to buy a gravel syphon with a protective plastic covering around it this will avoid the betta fish from getting syphoned into it. One of the best selling in Amazon and Amazon's choice which I found is this gravel cleaner. Gravel Cleaner.



Anti Chlorine:



This is also one of the most needed item a betta fish owner must have because too much chlorine in your betta fish tank can stress your fish and it can also damage the gills of your betta fish.

This is becoming more essential these days because our tap water is being treated with more chlorine these days so if we do not remove that chlorine from the tap water then it would be harmful for our betta fish. Here is another informative video from Girl Talks Fish.



So after watching this video we got to know that removing chlorine from our aquarium water is the best way to save our fish from chlorine poisioning. One of the most famous water De-Chlorinator used by many aquarists is the API Water conditioner. It is also Amazon's Choice. You can purchase it from Amazon through this link API Water Conditioner.




Aquarium Heater:


It is better to have a heater in a betta fish tank so that temperature does not suddenly change in our aquarium tank if the temeprature changes very suddenly in our aquarium tank then it can stress out our betta fish. This would lead to diseases so it is the best to have heater for our betta tank to maintain a constant temperature in our aquarium.

This is not the only reason why you need to get a heater for your betta fish tank there is an other reason too. Betta fish live in warmer temepratures in their wild habitats. In their wild habitat the temperature of water ranges from about 70 to 80 degrees Farenheit.

If you are living in cold countries like the United States and European countries then it is essential to have a heater for your tank because the room temeperature would be very low for your betta fish it can stress them out but some betta fishes learn to adapt to the temeprature but this does not happen always if you are living in the Asian countries then it is not neccessary to have a heater in your betta tank. One of the best heater found in Amazon is the fluval heater for aquariums Fluval E Electronic Heater




Another reason why water heater is always useful to have with you is that it can be very useful while curing some external parsite diseases that betta fish gets we can raise the temeprature of the aquarium water in this high temeprature it would be hard for the parasite to survive and there is a high chance that your betta fish will get cured.
