About Me

Here are all the details you may want to know about me and my blogging journey

Amal Shaji

Software Engineer & Aquarist

Hi Everyone ! I am Amal Shaji from India but I work and live in UAE(United Arab Emirates). I am a software engineer by profession. I've grown and breed many varieties of betta fishes throughout my life. My hobbies are article writing, playing badminton and watching movies.

I've had the desire to do blogging long back during my college days but I could never get enough time to do so due to studies. Finally I have decided to launch my own blog since I get more time now to dedicate and work into content writing for a blog. All the articles in this blog are written by me.

I have done my degree and graduation from Indian Institute Of Technology Roorkee. I have made this entire blog from scratch including all the coding without using any modern blogging applications like blogger, wordpress etc. Other than that I am an avid reader and a pet lover in general.
